About Nest Of Hope Support

Nest Of Hope Advocacy and Support Community was founded out of experience and passion by a cancer survivor, as an Advocacy and Support group. The organization aims among other things to advocate for patients, raise awareness about the cancer burden, as well in directing public and private efforts and resources into cancer control.

The organization has chosen to be part of strategic awareness campaign towards eliminating the fear and misconceptions, to enlighten the public on cancer prevalence, control, treatment options, care and management.

We are dedicated to ensuring that all people impacted by cancer are empowered by knowledge, strengthened by action and sustained by the community.

Our vision is to collaborate and work with the authorities and policy makers in order to generate a system that works for our environment in order to reduce the frequent loss of lives.

Nest Of Hope as a patient based advocacy organization is committed to providing support for cancer patients, families and caregivers. The organization serves as a platform to help beat the stigma associated with cancer, while offering hope and resources to those affected.
